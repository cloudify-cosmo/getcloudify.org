<p><b>Have you been looking to take advantage of the promise of the cloud only to discover that migrating your app to the cloud is a complex mess?</b></p>

h2. No more! We invite you to <i>Take the Cloudify Challenge</i>! 

<p>Cloudify enables you to take any app – no matter what its stack to the cloud of your choice – with ZERO code changes.</p>
<br>
<p>Sound too good to be true? Then by all means, come out and see it in action.</p>
<br>
<p>Join GigaSpaces and Alcatel-Lucent us who are jointly sponsoring the IGTCloud Summit Israel - A DLD Event, and enter your application in the Cloudify challenge.</p>
<br>
<p>One application will be selected by a panel of judges, combined with your votes, to be demoed live on <a href="http://www.alcatel-lucent.com/new-thinking/market-growth/cloud.html">Alacatel-Lucent's CloudBand</a> - a new class of carrier cloud that leverages the power of Cloudify - at an upcoming IGTCloud event.</p>
<br>
<p><b>If we lose</b>(that is we <i>just can't</i> Cloudify your app)<b> – you win! We’ll give you a prize!</p> 
<br>
<p>If we win – you win!</b></p>

<p>All you need to do is visit this <a href="https://cloudifysource.zendesk.com/forums/21371548-take-the-cloudify-challenge-application-submissions">CloudifySource Forum</a> to submit your application - and make sure it's <b><i>voted up</b></i>.</p>

<p>The app that has the most votes, and is qualified by our estemeed panel of judges, will be the app selected to be demoed at an upcoming event.</p>
