Cloudify Orchestration Workshop - NYC
27-29 July 2015

GigaSpaces invites you to join us for a free, hands-on Cloudify orchestration workshop!

In this workshop you will learn how to build a robust continuous delivery pipeline by standardizing deployment, orchestration and build processes to create smoother transitions between development and production environments.
