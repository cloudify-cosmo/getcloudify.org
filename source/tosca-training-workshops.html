Two Day Workshop

Introduction to TOSCA & Cloud Native Orchestration

 Register


Learn what is quickly becoming the de-facto standard for Cloud and NFV Applications

This intense two day workshop is the first of its kind to cover the fundamentals of TOSCA language and how it can be implemented for cloud orchestration using the Cloudify project. The workshop is led by one of our professional TOSCA instructors.

TOSCA, the “Topology and Orchestration Specification for Cloud Applications”, is rapidly becoming the de-facto standard for cloud application and NFV abstraction language and is supported by industry leaders such as IBM, Intel, CA, Redhat, GigaSpaces, Cisco and more, TOSCA is an open project and is managed and governed by the Oasis consortium.

Who is the workshop for?

This course is intended for anyone who is looking to get a better understanding of TOSCA and how it can be used in the real world. Typically these would be System Engineers, DevOps, Network Engineers, Application Developers, and Operations Managers.

Upon Completion You Will:

  • Understand the basics of the TOSCA language and how it's used
  • Be able to develop your own TOSCA blueprints
  • Deploy and use TOSCA blueprints with Cloudify Orchestration
