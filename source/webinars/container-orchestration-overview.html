How to Run Enterprise Production Workloads in the Cloud

November 2016 09:30am PDT | 12:30pm EDT

 Register



Register and learn why a Hybrid Cloud solution is a must have

In this webinar we'll show you what a no-compromises hybrid cloud can look like, and help explain how you can avoid the various pain points that you are having with your current cloud setup. We'll show you what a componentized tool chain that can already fit your enterprise applications, can look like.

We’ll do a deep dive into why your current cloud strategy isn't working, and show a live demo of how a hybrid cloud environment should and can work like.

You’ll learn

  • How to use a tool chain that fits the current way you work
  • How to leave existing IT silos behind and unlock your developer productivity
  • Increase software release cycles, yet still reduce CI/CD and DevOps risks at the same time
  • Life Pro tip: Why Your Cloud Failed (Hint: It's Not You!)

Duration: 1 hour
