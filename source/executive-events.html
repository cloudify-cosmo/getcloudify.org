Vassil Avramov is the CTO of Risk Focus a specialist firm delivering custom solutions to Capital Markets clients based in NYC.  Vassil began his career in Silicon Valley as a developer in technology startups. Prior to Risk Focus, he worked for a Risk Management vendor serving Sell-Side Capital Markets clients where he held a senior client engagement role. Now at RiskFocus, Vassil's expertise focuses on enabling enterprises, especially in the financial services sector, to embrace cloud technologies.



Nati Shalom, Founder and CTO at GigaSpaces, is a thought leader in Cloud Computing and Big Data Technologies. Shalom was recognized as a Top Cloud Computing Blogger for CIOs by The CIO Magazine and his blog is listed as an excellent blog by YCombinator, as well as the HP Helion MVP Award for his contributions to the OpenStack community. Shalom is the founder and also one of leaders of OpenStack Israel group, and is a frequent presenter at industry conferences.



Shay Hassidim, Deputy CTO and Distinguished Engineer at GigaSpaces, has over 20 years of experience in the software industry. Shay is part of the product leadership team at Gigaspaces and oversees the technical activities of the United States operation. Shay has in-depth technical experience with diverse technologies including Cloud Computing, Distributed Caching, SOA, PaaS, IaaS, and has been involved with projects in verticals: from Financial Services to Telecommunications, working with multinational Fortune 500 companies.
