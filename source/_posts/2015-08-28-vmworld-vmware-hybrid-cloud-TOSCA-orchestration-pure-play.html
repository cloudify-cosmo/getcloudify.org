Ah, VMworld. A place for IT pros and thought leaders to come together and talk virtualization and cloud technology. This year, the name of the game is “Ready for Any,” showcasing the VMware ecosystem for fast and secure architectures and unified hybrid cloud that lets users “innovate like a startup and execute like an enterprise.”

And this notion of hybrid cloud is as poignant as ever, with more enterprises extending their existing workloads across a VMware stack, or between different clouds altogether. Everyone wants to function like the Google’s, Facebook’s and Netflix’s of the world, running deployments seamlessly across a myriad of environments without a hitch.

Embrace change.

Over the last few months we’ve been working with VMware to simplify how vCloud Air users deploy applications across the VMware stack or into hybrid environments using Cloudify’s pure-play, open source, TOSCA standard-based orchestration.
